About us

Our story


The portal operates via multiple online platforms including a desktop website and various mobile solutions including Apple and Android applications.

Via our daily interactions with the entire landscape of trusted professional motor dealers and leading motoring manufacturer brands, we provide tools and content that enable Irish consumers to confidently research the second largest purchase they are ever likely to make - a car.

This activity culminates in just under 1 Million serious buyers and sellers coming together on the platform on a monthly basis. During 2015 over 110 million searches for used cars were carried out on Carzone.

Carzone is not only Ireland's Ultimate Motoring Marketplace, it's also an authority in the used car market with incredible insight into consumer buying trends which is used to support both Trade and Consumer requirements.
